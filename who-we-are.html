Who We Are

Our Mission

The Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers (SASE) is dedicated to helping students in STEM achieve their full career potential while contributing to their communities.

What is SASE?

SASE is a national organization with over 100 chapters across four regions in the United States: Northeast, Midwest, West, and South. The organization focuses on professional development, community building, and service.

What Makes SASE NYU Unique

SASE NYU provides a close-knit community where students can grow professionally, build friendships, and celebrate Asian culture while pursuing STEM careers.

Our Chapter Pillars

Professionalism

Building career and leadership skills through networking and mentorship.

Diversity

Creating an inclusive space that values different backgrounds.

Service

Giving back through outreach and community initiatives.
